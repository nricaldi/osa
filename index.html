Life at OSA

Master Stout:

Master Stout has been teaching Martial Arts for over 30+ years. He holds the ranking of 6th Dan. Master Stout earned a B.S. in Neurophysiology. Master Stout has taught Private Classes, Group Classes, and Corporate classes. His goal is to help his students achieve their goals and evolve beyond their potential as individuals and martial artists.

Instructor Hernandez:

Instructor Hernandez brings knowledge working with at-risk youth, children with disabilities, managing and supervising camps, developing and managing Instructors and Instructor Training.

Instructor Sethi:

Instructor Sethi brings knowledge working with at-risk youth, teen centers and therapeutic recreation with Fairfax County since 1996. Instructor Sethi earned a B.A. in Sociology/Anthropology with a concentration in French. She has a Masters Degree in Social Work. Instructor Sethi has long-standing experience working with IEPs, adaptive exercises, and inclusion programs.

Martial Arts
